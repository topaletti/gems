Europe has a lot to offer

Index funds and other similar ETFs are a fine investment,
but if you're like us, you want to take more personal control
and invest in specific companies you have high hopes for.
We're here to help you find them.

Our Database

We browse stock exchanges all over Europe, looking for strong performers with further growth potential. Our current criteria are valuation (between 500 million and 5 billion euros) and five-year stock performance (at least 50%). A lot of the companies we end up with already are global players, others are on their way there.
The database is freely accessible and currently contains around 200 companies, so take a look and see if you can find an investment opportunity that fits your interests.

The Gem Report

For more in-depth information on the companies in our database and the surrounding markets we're planning on regularly releasing articles with additional analysis in our Gem Report.
These reports will be available right here on this website and as an email newsletter.

The Community

A proper community makes things more fun, so we're trying to come up with interesting ways to create such a community. A simple chat room could be a start. Never underestimate the fun that can be had in a simple chat room! Another option would be friendly portfolio rivalries. Could be interesting to have your own virtual stock portfolio on this website and see how it performs over time compared to other people's portfolios.
We'll see what we can come up with. Stay tuned!
