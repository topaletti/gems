Europe has a lot to offer

Index funds and other similar ETFs are a fine investment,
but if you're like us, you want to take more personal control
and invest in specific companies you have high hopes for.
We're here to help you find them.

Our Company Database

We browse stock exchanges all over Europe, looking for strong performers with further growth potential. Our current selection criteria are valuation (between 500 million and 5 billion euros) and five-year stock performance (at least 50%). A lot of the companies we end up with already are global players, others are on their way there.
The company database is freely accessible and currently contains around 250 companies, so take a look and see if you can find an investment opportunity that fits your interests.

Our Forecast Challenges

A proper community makes things more fun, so we're always trying to come up with interesting new ways of creating such a community. Our forecast challenges are one such way. Taking into account current events, we prepare a new stock price forecast challenge every month. Make your forecast, update it when new information comes to light, and discuss your ideas with other users.
